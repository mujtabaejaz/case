## Content Audit Findings

The initial audit revealed several issues:

- **No keyword strategy**: Posts targeted random topics without search intent research
- **Thin content**: Average post was 800 words with surface-level coverage
- **No internal linking**: Posts existed in isolation, no topic clusters
- **Inconsistent voice**: Different authors, no style guide

## Strategy Framework

I developed a content strategy framework based on:

1. **Audience research**: Interviewed 15 customers and prospects
2. **Keyword research**: Identified 500+ relevant keywords by intent
3. **Competitive gap analysis**: Found underserved topics competitors missed
4. **Content mapping**: Aligned content types to buyer journey stages

## Editorial Guidelines

Created comprehensive guidelines covering:

- Brand voice and tone
- Writing style (active voice, plain language)
- SEO best practices
- Technical accuracy standards
- Visual content requirements
- Publishing workflow

These guidelines enabled consistent quality as the team scaled.
