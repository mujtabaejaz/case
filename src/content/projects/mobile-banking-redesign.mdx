## Design System Creation

As part of this project, I established a design system for the bank's digital products. This included:

- **Component library** with 50+ reusable components
- **Design tokens** for colors, typography, and spacing
- **Interaction patterns** documented with examples
- **Accessibility guidelines** for the design team

The design system has since been adopted across the bank's web platform and internal tools.

## Key Learnings

Working with a regulated industry taught me the importance of understanding compliance requirements early. Several initial design concepts had to be revised due to regulatory requirements around displaying financial information.

The collaboration with the development team was crucial. Weekly design-dev syncs helped identify technical constraints early and find creative solutions together.
