## Product Discovery

The discovery phase revealed a key insight: sellers were drowning in data but starving for insights. They could see their sales numbers but didn't understand *why* performance changed.

This shifted our approach from "build a dashboard" to "build an insights engine."

## Prioritization Framework

Used RICE scoring to prioritize features:

| Feature | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | Score |
|---------|-------|--------|------------|--------|-------|
| Performance insights | High | High | Medium | High | 1.5 |
| Traffic analytics | High | Medium | High | Medium | 2.0 |
| Competitor benchmarks | Medium | High | Low | Medium | 1.0 |

Traffic analytics shipped first due to higher confidence and lower effort.

## Launch Strategy

1. **Alpha** (2 weeks): Internal testing with fake seller accounts
2. **Beta** (4 weeks): 50 hand-picked sellers, weekly feedback sessions
3. **Limited GA** (2 weeks): Top 10% of sellers by GMV
4. **Full GA**: All sellers with in-app education

The phased approach let us catch issues early and build confidence.

## Stakeholder Management

This project required alignment across:

- **Engineering**: Capacity planning, technical feasibility
- **Data Science**: Analytics models, insight algorithms
- **Design**: User research, interface design
- **Marketing**: Launch communications, seller education
- **Sales**: Positioning, competitive differentiation

Weekly cross-functional syncs kept everyone aligned throughout.
