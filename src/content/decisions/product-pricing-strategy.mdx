## Research Phase

Before deciding, we conducted extensive research:

- **Customer interviews**: 40 customers across segments
- **Churn analysis**: Why did customers leave?
- **Win/loss analysis**: Why did deals close or not?
- **Competitive analysis**: How do competitors price?

Key finding: Customers who used the product more were happier and stayed longer. Per-seat pricing penalized heavy usage.

## The New Model

**Platform Fee** (monthly)
- Starter: $99/mo (up to 10 users)
- Professional: $499/mo (unlimited users)
- Enterprise: Custom (unlimited + SLA)

**Usage Component**
- Based on "active records" processed
- Included allowance with each tier
- Overage charged per 1,000 records

## Migration Strategy

We couldn't just flip the switch:

1. **Announce 6 months early**: Give customers time to plan
2. **Grandfather pricing**: Existing customers keep old pricing for 12 months
3. **Migration incentives**: Early adopters get 20% discount
4. **Usage calculator**: Tool to estimate new costs
5. **Dedicated support**: Migration specialists for enterprise

## Results After 6 Months

- **Enterprise ACV**: +40% (no more seat negotiations)
- **Small team conversion**: +25% (lower barrier to entry)
- **Net revenue retention**: 115% (expansion from usage growth)
- **Customer satisfaction**: NPS stable (no negative impact)

## Lessons for Pricing Changes

1. **Over-communicate**: Customers hate surprises
2. **Provide tools**: Calculators, usage dashboards
3. **Be generous in transition**: Short-term revenue hit for long-term trust
4. **Train your team**: Sales, support, success all need to understand deeply
